fastLoad

Tired of scene loading taking forever because Maya insists on caching the newly loaded textures?

Try the new and amazing -- ‘fastLoad’!

Just map ‘fastLoad’ to the hotkey you use to open a scene (typically ‘CTRL-o’) and watch your load times shrink to almost nil!

How it works:

Before loading and displaying the new scene, ‘fastLoad’ closes the MultiLister window and changes all panels to wireframe. The result? No textures! Maya draws the new geometry lickety-split!

Wait.. there's more!

‘fastLoad’ always remembers the last directory you used, and takes you to that same directory the next time you use ‘fastLoad’.

Does it get any better than this?? Well, actually, no.. that's really about it.
