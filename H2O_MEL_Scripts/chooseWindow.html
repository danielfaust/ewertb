chooseWindow

Function

A quick way to open some of the common Maya Windows, including:

Attribute Editor
Connection Editor
DopeSheet
Graph Editor
Hypergraph
Multilister
Outliner
Expression Editor


You may ask why I would want or need this script, since, for the most part, it merely duplicates the functions of several HotBox menu items. Well, I personally find it quicker to pop up this window via a hotkey (I have it mapped to 'CTRL-w') than to navigate through a couple layers of HotBox menus... It's all a matter of personal taste.
