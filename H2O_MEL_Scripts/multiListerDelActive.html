multiListerDelActive

Function

When the MultiLister is in Highlight Mode it requires a trip to the menus to delete a Light, Material or Shading Group. I wanted a quick button on the Tools Shelf to quickly turf a highlighted item, so I wrote this script. (It was also a good learning experience for navigating the MultiLister via MEL.)

Usage

multiListerDelActive;

The highlighted items in the MultiLister are deleted.
